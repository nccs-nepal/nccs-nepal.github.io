Past projects

NCCS researchers have led or been a part of studies across various domains of health and cognition.

Speech comprehension in adverse listening conditions: See Bhandari et al. (2024), Bhandari et al. (2022), Bhandari et al. (2021), Bhandari (2022)

Psychological response to covid-19 pandemic: See Lieberoth et al. (2021)

Executive control in bilinguals: See Bhandari et al. (2020), Mishra et al. (2019)

Neurodiversity: Bhandari & Khanal (2016)
